About

Hello, I'm William Wei, a Jakarta-based artist working across environment art, concept art, illustration, 3D, and motion. My work is driven by worldbuilding. I love exploring and creating stories, building the characters and the environments. My thirst in exploring leads me into various art styles and mediums, hence fitting the role of a generalist.

Two years into a professional generalist career, I'm narrowing toward environment and concept work while keeping the broader skillset visible. While I am confident in my skills as a generalist, I am more well-known amongst my peers for my leadership skills, taking art director and producer roles since university days, and being trusted as project leads throughout my professional career.
